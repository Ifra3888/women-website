Common Cyber Threats

Understanding these threats is the first step toward protection

Cyberbullying

Harassment, humiliation, or threats through digital platforms, often targeting women specifically.

Identity Theft

Stealing personal information to impersonate or commit fraud using someone else's identity.

Online Stalking

Persistent monitoring and harassment through digital means, creating fear and insecurity.

Phishing

Deceptive attempts to obtain sensitive information by disguising as trustworthy entities.

Online Safety Tips

Practical steps to protect yourself in the digital world

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique, complex passwords for each account and consider using a password manager.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your accounts with 2FA wherever possible.

Be Cautious with Personal Information

Think carefully before sharing personal details online and adjust privacy settings.

Recognize and Report Abuse

Learn to identify online harassment and know how to report it to platform administrators.

Secure Your Connections

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions and use a VPN when necessary.

Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your devices and applications to protect against security vulnerabilities.
